Reg v.1

Board Game Assistant

Overview

Reg, taking inspiration from such home assistants as Amazon's Alexa, is designed to be your personal assistant for all of your board games. When given an image of a rulebook, Reg can respond to your questions about the corresponding game's rules whenever you say "Hey Reg!" As version 1 of Reg, he is fairly limited in his ability to read the rules and respond to questions, but he will continue to be improved as time goes on.

How it works

To work, Reg needs two things, a PDF file of the rules for any games you'd like his help with, and access to a microphone. First, Reg converts the PDFs of any rules you've given him into plain text so that he can read them better. From there, he waits in a listening loop until he hears his name, at which point he will prompt you for a question. Once a response is given, he will look for a section in the rulebook text that has a title that matches one of the words in your question, and reads the following information about it to you.
